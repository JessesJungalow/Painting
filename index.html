About the artist

Hi, I'm Jesse —
welcome to the Jungalow

I'm a self-taught painter based in Cebu City, Philippines. My work is inspired by the lush greens, warm earth, and vibrant life of the tropics. Every piece I make is original — painted by hand, never printed.

I take commissions too — if you have an idea, let's make it happen.
